Our Story

Starlight Academy was founded with a vision to provide high-quality education that empowers students to become confident, responsible, and successful members of society.

Excellence Since 2010

For over a decade, Starlight Academy has maintained a reputation for academic excellence, discipline, innovation, and holistic student development.
